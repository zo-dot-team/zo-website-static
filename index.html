zo is a small co-operative team of designers & developers working on open and decentralised technology.

We value simplicity in every aspect of our work, like the tools we use, the code we write, the experiences we build, and the relationships we maintain.

We strive to build from the bottom-up. We iterate based on end users’ needs and experiences. We do not “move fast and break things”.

We coordinate in a peer-to-peer fashion. Instead of hierarchy, we emphasise better communication and cooperation. We get involved in projects as peers, not as human resources.

We use free/libre tools, open standards, and decentralised protocols as much as possible. We like to experiment with new governance models, decentralised infrastructure, etc.

We work openly and strive to document and share every step of the process in order to help others who may wish to iterate on our work.

We live inside our experiments, using what we build on a daily basis.
